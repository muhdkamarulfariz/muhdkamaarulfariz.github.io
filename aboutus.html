About Us

SportsHub is a Malaysian independent sports retailer specializing in the sale of athletic footwear, apparel, and equipment for running, football, and badminton. The enterprise has performed exceptionally well over time. Since its inception, the company has evolved from a single sports convenience concept to a multi-category, multi-concept sporting goods business with a clearly segmented strategy to appeal to consumers and sports enthusiasts of varying income levels by providing global brands at affordable prices.
